# Pun Lim Animation Storyboard

## Story Text
> "24-year-old Pun Lim is desperately clinging to a wooden plank in the vast emptiness of the Atlantic Ocean. Just moments earlier, he had been a steward aboard the SS Ben Lamond, a British merchant marine ship on its way to New York. Without warning, two massive explosions ripped through the vessel and Lim was swept overboard."

## Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: The Aftermath
**Duration**: ~150 frames (5 seconds)
**Text Overlay**: "24-year-old Pun Lim is desperately clinging to a wooden plank in the vast emptiness of the Atlantic Ocean."
**Visuals**:
- **Background**: Dark, infinite blue ocean with subtle wave animations.
- **Character**: Pun Lim, looking wet and exhausted, clinging to a floating wooden plank.
- **Camera**: Slowly zooming out to emphasize the "vast emptiness" of the ocean.

### Scene 2: The Flashback (Steward Life)
**Duration**: ~150 frames (5 seconds)
**Text Overlay**: "Just moments earlier, he had been a steward aboard the SS Ben Lamond, a British merchant marine ship on its way to New York."
**Visuals**:
- **Background**: Interior of a 1940s ship cabin or a deck view.
- **Character**: Pun Lim dressed in a neat steward's uniform, looking calm.
- **Animation**: A transition from the chaotic ocean back to the peaceful ship. Perhaps carrying a tray to show his job.

### Scene 3: The Explosions
**Duration**: ~150 frames (5 seconds)
**Text Overlay**: "Without warning, two massive explosions ripped through the vessel and Lim was swept overboard."
**Visuals**:
- **Background**: The SS Ben Lamond on the ocean.
- **Animation**: Sudden bright orange/red flashes (explosions). The screen shakes violently using a spring animation.
- **Action**: Pun Lim is thrown backward/overboard, spinning as he falls into the water.
- **Transition**: Fades back into the dark ocean, looping back to the setting of Scene 1.

## Required Assets
1. **Pun Lim Character**: Reusable SVG character with changeable expressions (exhausted/distressed vs. calm steward).
2. **Wooden Plank**: Simple SVG prop for Scene 1.
3. **Ocean Background**: Gradient/wave animated background.
4. **Ship/Explosion Vectors**: Simple ship silhouette and explosion graphics for Scene 3.
