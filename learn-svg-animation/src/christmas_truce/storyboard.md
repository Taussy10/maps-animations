# Christmas Truce of 1914 - Storyboard

## Script
"December 1914. In the middle of World War I, British and German soldiers were hiding in freezing trenches, just meters apart. Then something unbelievable happened. Someone started singing a Christmas song. Soon, soldiers from both sides climbed out of their trenches, shook hands, exchanged gifts, and even played football together. For one day, enemies became friends. The next morning, the war continued. But for a few hours, humanity won."

## Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: The Freezing Trenches
**Frames:** 0 - 150 (5 seconds)
**Narration:** "December 1914. In the middle of World War I, British and German soldiers were hiding in freezing trenches, just meters apart."
**Visuals:** 
- A cold, dark blue snowy night background.
- Two dirt trenches on opposite sides of the screen.
- A British soldier (left) and a German soldier (right) peeking out.
- Subtle SVG animations: snow falling, characters shivering slightly (using Remotion `interpolate` and `Math.sin` driven by `useCurrentFrame()`).

### Scene 2: The Song
**Frames:** 150 - 270 (4 seconds)
**Narration:** "Then something unbelievable happened. Someone started singing a Christmas song."
**Visuals:** 
- Zoom slightly into the center/right side.
- Glowing musical notes float up from the German trench.
- The British soldier stops shivering and looks up in surprise.

### Scene 3: Coming Together
**Frames:** 270 - 450 (6 seconds)
**Narration:** "Soon, soldiers from both sides climbed out of their trenches, shook hands, exchanged gifts, and even played football together."
**Visuals:** 
- Both soldiers rise out of their trenches.
- They walk to the center of "No Man's Land".
- They shake hands.
- A football drops in, and they playfully kick it back and forth.

### Scene 4: The Aftermath
**Frames:** 450 - 600 (5 seconds)
**Narration:** "For one day, enemies became friends. The next morning, the war continued. But for a few hours, humanity won."
**Visuals:** 
- The night sky fades into morning light (colors shift from dark blue to orange/light blue).
- The soldiers wave goodbye and descend back into their respective trenches.
- Text fades in at the center: "Humanity won."
