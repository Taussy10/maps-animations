# The Great Book-Chest Escape (1621) - Fast Storyboard (16s)

A 16-second historical animation story. The scene changes every **2 seconds (60 frames @ 30fps)** to keep the action fast and engaging.

## Storyboard Timeline

| Time | Frames | Scene | Visual Action | Narration / Subtext |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0s – 2s** | `0 – 60` | **Scene 1: Locked Up** | Hugo Grotius stands crying behind jail bars. The Guard stands outside keeping watch. | *"Hugo Grotius is sentenced to life in castle prison."* |
| **2s – 4s** | `60 – 120` | **Scene 2: The Idea** | A lightbulb pops up above Hugo's head. He stops crying and smiles. | *"But he gets a brilliant idea."* |
| **4s – 6s** | `120 – 180` | **Scene 3: The Chest Arrives** | Hugo's wife delivers a large chest of "books" to the cell door. Guard inspects it. | *"His wife delivers a chest of books to his cell."* |
| **6s – 8s** | `180 – 240` | **Scene 4: Inside the Chest** | The chest lid opens. Hugo climbs in and the lid closes. | *"Hugo climbs inside the empty chest instead."* |
| **8s – 10s** | `240 – 300` | **Scene 5: Heavy Lifting** | The Guard lifts the chest. Sweat drops fly off the Guard (struggling with the weight). | *"The guard lifts the chest, complaining it is heavy."* |
| **10s – 12s** | `300 – 360` | **Scene 6: Escaping the Gates** | The Guard carries the chest down the prison hallway past the cell gates. | *"He carries it right past the main gates."* |
| **12s – 14s** | `360 – 420` | **Scene 7: Left Outside** | The chest is set down outside the castle. The Guard walks away, dusting his hands off. | *"They leave the chest outside on the dock."* |
| **14s – 16s** | `420 – 480` | **Scene 8: Freedom!** | The chest lid pops open. Hugo jumps out, smiles, and runs off-screen. | *"Hugo pops out and escapes to freedom!"* |
